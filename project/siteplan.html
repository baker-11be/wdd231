Baker City Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Baker City Chamber of Commerce

This name communicates a local business network for Baker City and reinforces the chamber's role as a trusted source for community events, member services, and economic growth.

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a central hub for Baker City business members, providing information about membership benefits, a searchable member directory, local news, and resources for both residents and visitors.

Scenarios

  • What local businesses offer services near the riverfront and how can I contact them?
  • How do I apply to become a chamber member and what are the available membership levels?
  • When is the next chamber event, and how can I register as a participant?
