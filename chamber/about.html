About the Chamber

Our mission is to support Kampala businesses with resources, skills, networking and advocacy.

Who We Are

The Kampala Chamber of Commerce brings together local businesses, entrepreneurs and community leaders to promote economic growth and sustainable development.

We host events, provide member services and connect businesses with customers and partners across the region.
